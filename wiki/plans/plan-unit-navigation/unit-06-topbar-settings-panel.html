Unit 06 - TopBar Settings Panel

Summary

  • Status: complete
  • Plan: extend the TopBar action group with clearer Projects affordance, a placeholder Settings panel, and vertical-only panel entry motion.
  • Blockers: none
  • Read: sed -n '1,140p' public/index.html, sed -n '120,240p' public/app.css, sed -n '130,280p' public/app.js

Intent

Make the TopBar action group feel complete enough for dogfooding by adding an icon to Projects, reserving a Settings surface, and simplifying panel motion so dialogs drop straight down from the bar.

Scope

  • Add a circle-pile icon to the Projects button.
  • Add a Settings button with a gear icon and placeholder panel UI.
  • Keep Projects, Up Next, and Settings mutually exclusive with existing outside-click and Escape close behavior.
  • Change shared TopBar panel entry animation from diagonal/top-left-feeling motion to vertical-only down motion.

Non-goals

  • No persistent settings storage.
  • No new project management behavior.
  • No broader workspace layout redesign.

Verification

  • pnpm run check passed on 2026-05-11.
  • pnpm run smoke:launch passed on 2026-05-11.
  • Agent-browser visual check passed on 2026-05-11: Settings opens as a compact top-right panel, Projects remains mutually exclusive, and the TopBar buttons fit without overlap.
