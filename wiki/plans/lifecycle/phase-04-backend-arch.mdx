## Phase goal

Choose the backend architecture (data model, auth, hosting, realtime/sync as needed) now that the UI mocks reveal the real data and interaction requirements — and record it as a durable decision.

## Entry gate

Phase 3 cleared (UI mocks exist, so the data requirements are concrete).

## Sub-agent contract

- **Skills**: `grill-with-docs` to stress-test options against the user stories and mocks.
- **Produces**: `wiki/architecture.mdx` with `Decision`/`DecisionOption` records for the chosen architecture and the alternatives considered.

<Verification>

- `wiki/architecture.mdx` records the selected architecture and why, grounded in the mocks' requirements.

</Verification>
