<h2>Intent</h2>
<p>Make the TopBar action group feel complete enough for dogfooding by adding an icon to Projects, reserving a Settings surface, and simplifying panel motion so dialogs drop straight down from the bar.</p>
<Scope>
<ul>
  <li>Add a circle-pile icon to the Projects button.</li>
  <li>Add a Settings button with a gear icon and placeholder panel UI.</li>
  <li>Keep Projects, Up Next, and Settings mutually exclusive with existing outside-click and Escape close behavior.</li>
  <li>Change shared TopBar panel entry animation from diagonal/top-left-feeling motion to vertical-only down motion.</li>
</ul>
</Scope>
<h2>Non-goals</h2>
<ul>
  <li>No persistent settings storage.</li>
  <li>No new project management behavior.</li>
  <li>No broader workspace layout redesign.</li>
</ul>
