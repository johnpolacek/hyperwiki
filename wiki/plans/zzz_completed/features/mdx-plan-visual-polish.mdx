<Decision title="Design Direction">
  <p>Use a refined operational brief style: strong title, compact metadata strip, left-ruled sections, restrained evidence/decision panels, and fewer full cards. Keep shadcn primitives where they carry behavior or semantic structure, but avoid dashboard-card repetition.</p>
</Decision>
