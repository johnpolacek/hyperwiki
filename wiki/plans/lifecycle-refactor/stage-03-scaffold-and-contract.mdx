## Stage Goal

The lifecycle is not a feature of this one repo — it is the default. New and imported projects are scaffolded with `wiki/plans/lifecycle/`, and `/api/project-contract` (plus its MCP surface) reports the active phase and gate status so agents never scrape rendered MDX.

<Dependencies>

- Stage 02 (the canonical lifecycle MDX shape these pages are seeded from).

</Dependencies>

<Scope>

- `src-tauri/src/domain/verification.rs`, `project_contract`. The MCP `get_project_contract` path inherits the change for free.

</Scope>

<ImplementationNotes>
