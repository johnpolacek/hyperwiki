## Stage Goal

`runCommandAction` handles `"orchestrate"` and `"lifecycle-phase"`, reusing the `"planning"` and `"execute"` archetypes — the orchestrator reports the active phase and blocking gate, and a phase handoff scopes a terminal to the phase's current work with the right skill referenced.

- Stage 01 (`CommandAction` values, `lifecycle.ts` derivation).
- Stage 03 (the contract's lifecycle block is the orchestrator's source of truth).

</Dependencies>

<Scope>

</Scope>

<ImplementationNotes>

- `phaseAgentPrompt(phaseId, state)` — per-phase brief: the phase goal, the repo-local skill(s) to load, the `childPlan` (or PRD target) to work in, and the exit gate.

</ImplementationNotes>
