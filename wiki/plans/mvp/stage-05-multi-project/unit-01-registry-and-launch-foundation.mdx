<h1>Unit 01 - Registry and Launch Foundation</h1>
<p><a href="/wiki/plans/mvp/stage-05-multi-project.mdx">Stage 05 - Multi-project Workspace</a></p>
<section className="summary">
  <h2>Summary</h2>
<ul>
    <li>Status: complete</li>
    <li>Add a user-level registry at <code>~/.hyperwiki/projects.json</code>.</li>
    <li><code>npx hyperwiki</code> from a hyperwiki-initialized repo auto-registers that repo and opens it as the active project.</li>
    <li>Only repos with <code>wiki/</code> and <code>.hyperwiki/config.json</code> are eligible.</li>
    <li>Project names come from config <code>projectName</code>, then package name, then folder name.</li>
    <li>Implemented with a user-level registry that can be isolated in tests through <code>HYPERWIKI_HOME</code>.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
