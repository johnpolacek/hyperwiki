<h2>Intent</h2>
<p>Make hyperwiki obvious to start. From a fresh dogfood session, one command should bring up the local wiki/ADE workspace without requiring the user to remember separate server, browser, and terminal steps.</p>
<h2>Dogfood Finding</h2>
<p>The first-use friction is launch ambiguity: hyperwiki is essentially a local LLM wiki site and ADE in one, but the current workflow does not clearly answer how to launch the whole environment.</p>
